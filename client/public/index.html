MapDeduce

A census project by Dima Smirnov, Ted Kornish,
Ziv Epstein, Antony Bello, and Noam Hurwitz.
Now, more than ever, we need to understand each other. Social science research relies on college students and Amazon Mechanical Turk workers for experiments, which compromises the representation of surveyed populations. MapDeduce provides an accessible way for researchers to analyze the scope of their data.
